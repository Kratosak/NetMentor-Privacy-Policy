# Privacy Policy for NetMentor: IT Network Mastery

**Last updated:** April 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **NetMentor: IT Network Mastery** ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our Android application.

---

## 1. Overview

NetMentor is a quiz application designed to help users prepare for IT certification exams. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to function with minimal data collection — we do not require user accounts, we do not collect personal information, and we do not operate any backend servers.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

| Data | Description | Personally Identifiable? |
|---|---|---|
| Quiz topics | Name, description, difficulty level | No |
| Quiz questions | Question text, answer options, correct answer, explanation | No |
| Quiz session results | Score and missed question IDs (held in memory during session only, not saved permanently) | No |

**We do not collect, store, or transmit:**
- Your name, email address, or any contact information
- Device identifiers (IMEI, IMSI, serial number)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Crash reports
- Any other personally identifiable information (PII)

### 2.2 Information Collected by Third Parties (Google AdMob)

The App displays banner advertisements served by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may automatically collect the following information to serve and measure ads:

- Device advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID / GAID)
- IP address (used to derive approximate location)
- Ad interaction data (impressions and clicks)
- Device information (OS version, screen size, language)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. It is governed by **Google's Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

To learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services, visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

---

## 3. How the Data Is Used

### 3.1 Locally Stored Data

Data stored on your device is used **solely** to:
- Present quiz questions and topics within the App
- Calculate and display your quiz score at the end of each session

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

### 3.2 AdMob Data

Data collected by Google AdMob is used by Google to:
- Serve banner advertisements within the App
- Measure ad performance and prevent ad fraud
- Personalize ads based on your interests (subject to your ad preferences)

We receive aggregated, non-identifiable ad revenue reports from Google. We do not receive any personally identifiable data from AdMob.

---

## 4. Internet Permission

The App declares one permission that involves network access:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `android.permission.INTERNET` | Required exclusively for loading Google AdMob banner advertisements |

The App itself does not transmit any user data over the internet. No data is sent to our servers because we do not operate any servers.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties, because we do not collect any personal information.

The only third party that may receive data when you use the App is **Google AdMob**, as described in Section 2.2.

---

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

### 6.1 App Data

All quiz data stored by the App resides on your device in a private SQLite database. It is not accessible to other apps or to us.

**To delete all App data:**
- Go to **Android Settings → Apps → NetMentor → Storage → Clear Data**, or
- **Uninstall the App**

### 6.2 AdMob / Google Advertising Data

To opt out of personalized advertising or to delete your advertising identifier:

- **Android 12 and later:** Go to **Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID**
- **Android 11 and earlier:** Go to **Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization**

For further control over Google's data collection, visit the Google My Account page: https://myaccount.google.com

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since the App collects no personal information from any user, it poses no special risk to children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details in Section 9.

---

## 8. Data Security

Since the App stores no personal data and operates no backend servers, there is no risk of a server-side data breach involving your personal information. All locally stored data (quiz content and session scores) is stored in the App's private directory on your device, which is not accessible to other applications.
